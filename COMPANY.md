# Vantyx

AI-orchestrated web-transformation agency for local home-services businesses (electricians, plumbers, HVAC, roofers, general contractors) in Ohio and adjacent markets.

## Mission

Use Paperclip-orchestrated AI agents to:

1. **Identify** under-served local businesses via the Lead Intelligence Engine — Google Maps (SerpAPI), Ohio new-LLC RSS, and neglected-website scoring (0–100 rubric).
2. **Demonstrate** value before the first sales call — the "Before We Even Spoke" pipeline rebuilds the prospect's homepage as a deployed Vercel preview at `<lead-slug>.preview.<vantyx-domain>`.
3. **Convert** previews into project + retainer revenue (hosting, SEO, blog content, GBP/reputation management, ads, AI chatbots, maintenance).

## Phase 1 goals

1. Stand up Board → CEO → C-suite → IC org tree (6 agents).
2. Ship the Lead Intelligence Engine: ≥20 scored leads in any 50-mile radius within 30 minutes of a Board-assigned territory task.
3. Ship the demo pipeline: ≥5 deployed previews per scored batch within 24h, under $15 external-API spend.
4. Establish the tiered escalation system (Section 13 of the plan) so the Board (Ryan) is alerted accurately when human input is needed — and not before.

## Non-goals (Phase 1)

- Not a Wix/Squarespace replacement — we build custom, AI-assisted, conversion-tuned sites.
- Not a generic "AI tool" SaaS — we're a services company that uses AI as force multiplication.
- Not chasing every lead — score ≥65 only; sub-65 leads are explicitly out of scope.
